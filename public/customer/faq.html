📦 Ordering

How do I place an order?

To place an order, follow these steps:
1. Browse products on the home page or shop page
2. Click on product to view details
3. Select size/variation if available
4. Click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now"
5. Go to cart and proceed to checkout
6. Add delivery address and confirm order
7. Select Cash on Delivery as payment method
8. Click "Place Order"

Can I modify my order after placing?

Order cannot be modified once placed. However, you can cancel the order within the cancellation window and place a new order. Use the cancel code provided to the rider at delivery.

What is a Cancel Code?

Cancel Code is a unique 6-digit code sent with your order via SMS/WhatsApp/Email. You need to provide this code to the rider if you wish to cancel the order at delivery. The code is valid for 48 hours from order placement.

💳 Payment & COD

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept only Cash on Delivery (COD). No online payments, UPI, or wallet payments are accepted for orders. You pay only when you receive the product.

What is Open Box Delivery?

Open Box Delivery allows you to open and check the product in front of the delivery rider before making payment. If you find any defect or are unsatisfied, you can return or exchange the product immediately.

Why is my COD blocked?

COD may be blocked due to multiple RTOs (Return to Origin) or order cancellations. Your trust score decreases with each RTO. To unblock, contact customer support and pay the unblock fine of ₹100.

🚚 Delivery & Tracking

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by going to "My Orders" section and clicking "Track Order" on your order. You can also use the tracking ID sent via SMS/WhatsApp on the tracking page.

What is the delivery time?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Delivery charge is ₹40, which is waived off for orders above ₹499.

Can I schedule delivery time?

Yes, you can select preferred delivery time slots during checkout - Morning (9-2), Evening (3-8), or Night (6-10).

🔄 Returns & Cancellation

How do I cancel my order?

To cancel your order, provide the 6-digit Cancel Code to the delivery rider. The order will be cancelled instantly and marked as RTO. Cancellation is only possible before the order reaches "Out for Delivery" status.

Can I return a product?

Yes, same-time return is available at delivery. Check the product in front of the rider and return immediately if not satisfied. After delivery, returns are accepted within 7 days. Return requests can be raised from the Returns section.

How does exchange work?

Exchange can be requested at delivery or within 7 days. Our rider will pick up the old product and deliver the new product. Any price difference will be collected in cash at the time of exchange.

💰 Wallet & Coins

What are Coins?

Coins are loyalty points where 1 Coin = ₹1. Earn coins by placing orders (2% cashback), writing reviews (10 coins), daily check-in (5 coins), and referring friends (50 coins). Coins can be used to pay for orders.

How do I claim wallet balance?

Go to Wallet section, click "Claim", enter the amount (minimum ₹100), and provide your UPI ID. Amount will be credited within 2-3 business days after TDS deduction (1%).

👤 Account

How do I update my profile?

Go to Account section, click "Edit Profile" to update your name, email, mobile number, and UPI ID for payouts.

How to delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. Your data will be deleted after 30 days as per GDPR compliance. You can also request account reactivation within this period.

How does Trust Score work?

Trust score starts at 100. It increases by +5 for successful orders and decreases by -20 for RTO/cancellations. Trust score below 50 may affect COD availability.
